Privacy Policy for Made in Libya for Manufacturers App

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

Welcome to Made in Libya for Manufacturers App (the "App," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Based on our declarations to app store providers, we may collect the following types of user data:

  • Account Creation Information: We primarily use OAuth (e.g., Sign in with Google) for account creation. Information from the OAuth provider may include your email address and a user identifier.
  • Personal Information:
    • Email Address (required for app functionality and account management).
    • Physical Address (optional, for app functionality and account management).
    • Phone Number (optional, for app functionality and account management).
    • Photos you upload (e.g., for product listings, profiles; required for app functionality and account management).
  • App Activity & Performance Information:
    • Crash Logs (optional, for analytics).
    • User Interactions with the App (required, for app functionality and analytics).
    • In-app Search History (required, for app functionality and analytics).

We do not knowingly collect other sensitive personal information unless directly provided by you for specific app features, and only with your consent where required.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

  • Providing and maintaining the App, including account creation and management.
  • Enabling core app functionalities like product listings, user interactions, and search.
  • Monitoring and analyzing usage to improve the App.
  • Diagnosing and fixing technical problems.
  • Providing users (e.g., manufacturers) with insights and analytics dashboards related to their activity on the App, using their specific data.
  • Generating aggregated and anonymized statistical data for internal analysis, platform trends, service improvement, and potentially providing benchmark insights to users. Such aggregated and anonymized data does not identify individual users.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We generally do not share your personal information with unrelated third parties for their independent use, except in the following circumstances:

  • User-to-User Visibility: Certain information you provide (e.g., manufacturer profiles, product listings) will be visible to other App users as part of the App's functionality.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting) who process your data on our behalf to help us operate and improve the App. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred.
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose with your explicit consent.

Aggregated and anonymized data may be used for platform analytics or benchmark insights and is not considered sharing of personal information.

4. Data Security

We declare that user data is encrypted in transit. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, for active accounts, to provide services (including analytics dashboards), or as required by law. Aggregated and anonymized data may be kept for longer periods.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

You have rights regarding your personal data, including access, correction, and deletion.

  • Account Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data via the link provided in our Data Safety section on the app store, or through app settings if available. Our account deletion instruction page is: https://srourganoush.github.io/mil/delete_request.html
  • Specific Data Deletion: You can request deletion of specific data without deleting your entire account via the link provided in our Data Safety section, or through in-app controls. Our specific data deletion instruction page is: https://srourganoush.github.io/mil/delete_part_data.html

Please contact us to exercise these rights. We may require identity verification.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 (or a higher age if stipulated by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page and/or through other means. Changes are effective when posted.
